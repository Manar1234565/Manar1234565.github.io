Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is widely regarded as one of the greatest football players of all time. The Argentine forward has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards and led his national team to victory in the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who has consistently competed with Messi for the title of world's best player. He has won five Ballon d'Or awards and has achieved success in England, Spain, and Italy.

Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé is a French forward considered the heir apparent to Messi and Ronaldo. His explosive speed, technical skill, and clinical finishing have made him one of the most exciting players in modern football.

Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian midfielder renowned for his exceptional passing range, vision, and technical ability. He is widely considered the best attacking midfielder in world football.

Erling Haaland

Erling Haaland is a Norwegian striker known for his incredible goal-scoring prowess and physical dominance. His combination of speed, strength, and finishing ability has made him one of the most feared attackers in football.

Virgil van Dijk

Virgil van Dijk is a Dutch defender considered one of the best center-backs in the world. His commanding presence, aerial ability, and leadership have transformed Liverpool's defense since his arrival.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah is an Egyptian winger known for his incredible speed, dribbling skills, and goal-scoring ability. He has been a key player for Liverpool, winning multiple Golden Boot awards in the Premier League.

Karim Benzema

Karim Benzema is a French striker who enjoyed a highly successful career at Real Madrid. Known for his technical ability, link-up play, and clinical finishing, he won the 2022 Ballon d'Or after an exceptional season.
